Servo Auger Filler Collar Type Pouch Packing Machine

High-precision filling for non-free flowing powders.

Machine Overview

Collar Type Auger Servo Pouch Packing machine is commonly used for filling and sealing non-free flowing powders those are sticky or contain some oil or moisture within like all spice powders also called masala as Red chilli powder or Lal mirch masala, Turmeric powder or haldi, Coriander or Dhaniya Powder, Garam Masala, Chiken Masala, Meat masala, Wheat flour Atta, gram flour Besan, corn starch or ararot, milk powder, heena powder, ayurvedic powder, chemical and pharmaceutical powders. This collar type center seal pouch packaging machine uses Auger as filler and servo motor to highly precise quantity to be filled. It is highly efficient with high speed, low power consumption and low rejection rate.
