About Shrijeta Global

SHRIJETA GLOBAL heartily welcomes you to our place of web and assures that every of your need regarding packaging will be fulfilled here whether you need machines or services. Known as one of top manufactures and exporters of Industrial packaging machines and equipments in India, we offer wide range of customized packing machinery for various food and non food products like powder, liquid, paste, granules etc in pouch, bag or carton box, as per requirement of our valuable customers and are known for most modern design, low maintenance, high performance and competitive price. We have team of highly qualified, experienced and dedicated people to achieve goals with accuracy.

Incorporated in the 2015 and based in Faridabad, Haryana, India, Shrijeta Global (formerly known as Shrijeta Pack Tech) has earned its name in a short time span due to well equipped research and development ability, commitment and quality automatic packing machines manufacturers and exporters in India. We have earned fame in the field of manufacturing and servicing high quality Lined Carton Machines, Collar Type Pouch Packing Machine also with auger and servo, Multi Track Packing Machines, Collar Type FFS Machine, Bagger System with Multihead weigher and Pouch Packing Machine Spare Parts. We are a sole proprietorship firm with huge work place. Inferable from the vast experience and excellent marketing strategies, we have possessed the capacity to achieve trust of our esteemed customers.

Our packing machinery is crafted by high quality material, latest technology and yes loyalty yet at best price.
