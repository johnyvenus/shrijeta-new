Frequently Asked Questions

What materials can your machines pack? +

We handle powders (free & non-free flowing), granules, liquids, pastes, snacks, dry fruits, and more. Machines are configurable with auger, servo piston, or multihead/linear weigher dosing.

Do you provide installation and training? +

Yes. Our engineers travel to your site for machine installation, calibration, and operator training. We also provide video manuals and 24/7 WhatsApp support.

What is the warranty period? +

All machines come with 18 months warranty on mechanical parts and 12 months on electrical components. Extended warranty and AMC plans available.

Can you customize machines for my product? +

Absolutely. We’ve customized fillers for turmeric, protein powder, honey, ghee, detergent, and even chili flakes. Share your sample — we’ll test and recommend the best solution.
