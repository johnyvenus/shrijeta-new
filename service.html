Service & Support

Installation and Training

Our commitment to you doesn't end at purchase. Our expert engineers travel to your site for complete machine installation, calibration, and comprehensive operator training. We ensure your team is fully equipped to run the machinery efficiently and safely from day one. We also provide detailed video manuals and maintain 24/7 WhatsApp support for any immediate queries.

Warranty and AMC

We stand by the quality of our machines. All our products come with a standard 18-month warranty on mechanical parts and a 12-month warranty on electrical components. To ensure long-term performance and peace of mind, we also offer extended warranty options and Annual Maintenance Contracts (AMCs). Our AMC plans are designed to provide regular preventive maintenance, minimizing downtime and maximizing your productivity.

Customization and Support

Every product is unique, and sometimes a standard machine isn't the perfect fit. We specialize in customizing our machines to meet your specific product requirements. Whether it's a particular type of powder, a unique liquid viscosity, or a specific pouch size, we can tailor a solution for you. Just share your product sample with us, and our technical team will conduct tests and recommend the best configuration for your needs.
